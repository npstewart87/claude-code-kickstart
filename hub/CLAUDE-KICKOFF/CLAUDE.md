# CLAUDE KICKOFF — Session Lobby

This folder is the starting point for every session. It is a LOBBY — no real
work happens here. Your job when a session starts here:

1. Greet briefly. List their projects from `~/projects/` (just the names, one
   line each, with the status line from each project's CLAUDE.md if present).
2. Ask what they want to do — continue one of those, or start something new.
3. **Continue:** `cd` into that project, read its CLAUDE.md + vault note, give
   a one-sentence "where we left off", and keep going.
4. **New:** follow the New Project Protocol from the global rules (copy
   `~/templates/new-project`, name it, `cd` there, work).

Do NOT create or edit files in this folder. If they ask for something tiny that
doesn't deserve a project (a quick question, a one-off calculation), just
answer it here without creating anything.

If they seem lost or asks "what can you do", give him this in plain words:
- "Tell me an idea and I'll build it with you" (websites, tools, documents, anything)
- "Ask me anything — research, writing, math, planning"
- "Say 'show my projects' to see what we've already got going"
- "Everything we do is saved here and in your Obsidian notes automatically"
